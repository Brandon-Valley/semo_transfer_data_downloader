Select an institution to see a list of evaluated course equivalencies. Click on the view icon next to each course to view detailed information about the equivalency. Course equivalencies may change at any time without notice and are continuously being developed and added to this database. For regionally accredited institutions, if the course is not listed, it will be reviewed for course equivalency at the time of transfer. For all courses that transfer, the number of credits accepted will be equivalent to the SEMESTER credits earned at the transfer institution. Courses taken through a community college, while transferable, will not grant senior division credit regardless of course number used in transfer. Southeast Missouri State University reserves the right to make changes in transfer equivalencies at any time.
